Sticky iOS 26 Liquid Glass top bar — translucent gold-tinted chrome that floats over scrolling flat content.

```jsx
<GlassNavBar
  subtitle="Tuesday · Jun 14"
  title="Today"
  large
  leading={<LogoMark />}
  trailing={<IconButton glass icon={<Bell />} />}
/>
```

`large` renders the bigger bottom-aligned title (iOS large-title style). Glass only — the content scrolling beneath stays flat.
