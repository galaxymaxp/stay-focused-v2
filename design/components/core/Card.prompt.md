Flat warm content surface — the default container for blocks, lists, and sheets. Content is always flat (reserve glass for chrome).

```jsx
<Card padding="md">…</Card>
<Card selected>Active block</Card>
<Card accent>Gently highlighted</Card>
```

`padding`: `none | sm | md | lg`. `selected` gives the gold-tinted active surface; `accent` is a softer tint.
